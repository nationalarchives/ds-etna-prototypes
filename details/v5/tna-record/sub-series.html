Title: REPORTS TO ADMIRALTY
Note: All "C" except for any subsequent reports on ship protection The reports on war researches, as issued, were anonymous, but contained in the reference number the initials of the author. The responsibility of the author for the report in its final form varied considerable. In most cases, as a member of the section of the Laboratory conducting the work, he produced the first draft or was responsible for the accuracy of the observations recorded. Frequently others, whose initials and names do not appear, contributed materially to the work. June 1945
